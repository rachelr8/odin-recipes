Lasagna

Have I ever made lasagna from scratch? No. I am hoping to try this recipe since the title does lead you to believe it is the "World's Best Lasagna". Lets hope it is as good as everyone says it is. Otherwise I will have wasted a whole ton of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4lb Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6z) cans tomato paste
  • 2 (6.5oz) cans tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teasoons dries basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 4 tablesppons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4lb mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. In a dutch oven, cook sausage, ground beef onion and garlic over medium heat until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, pepper and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8-10 minutes. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 2 tablespoons parsley and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Preheat oven to 350°
  4. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups sauce in the bottom of a 9X13 baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture and top with a third of mozarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups sauce over mozarella and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozarella and parmesan. cover with foil.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool 15 minutes before serving
